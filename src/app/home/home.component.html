Angular 6 Firestore Project

This is a simple project in terms of business logic, but covers most of the key angular tools, concepts and design patterns, such as Multiple modules structure, Lazy Loading, Routing and Child Routing, Resolvers, Observables, Firebase CRUD, Pipe filters, Angular Material, Reactive forms, AOT compilation and others.
